About Our External Partnership Program

Carnegie Mellon University’s Dietrich College of Humanities and Social Sciences offers many resources to both our students and our local communities. With a team dedicated to assisting you, those that want to get in contact with Dietrich College students and faculty can do so with ease.

Partnership Success Stories

It is through the combined effort of our partners are we able to better the world around us. Over the years, Dietrich College has worked with many different groups and organizations. By having our students build relations with people outside of the school, we offer a place to prepare them for life in a future career.

When we work together, projects can achieve more than we can imagine. Bringing in students and faculty offer new perspectives on problems, each individual bringing their own cummulative knowledge to the table. Some of our best work has come from collaborative efforts. Community members and those outside of our circle come together to make something new.
